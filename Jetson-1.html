Run YOLO V5 on Jetson nano step by step tutorial

And this is my Jetson nano with 4GB memory:

What you need:

1. A Jetson nano (If you are using other Jetson developer kit from Nvidia, I am sure you can take this as reference as well.)

2. A SD card and power suply which meets the needs.

3. Another computer or laptop with internet connection and built-in SD card slot or an adapter.

4. A monitor with HDMI port, a USB mouse and a USB keyboard.

Step by step:

Assume that you just got your new Jetson nano, you need to write image to the microSD card. At this step, please go to check Nvidia's official instructions.

After logging in, you can see your Jetson nano is running ubuntu 18.04 LTS system.
